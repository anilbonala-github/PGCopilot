Privacy Policy for PGCopilot

Last updated: 24 June 2026

PGCopilot is a Hostel/PG management application for hostel and PG owners, staff, and managers. It helps manage hostels, rooms, beds, tenants, documents, rent billing, payments, expenses, and reports.

Information We Collect

PGCopilot may collect or store information entered by hostel owners or authorised staff, including:

  • Owner or staff mobile number used for OTP login.
  • Hostel details such as name, address, contact number, rooms, and beds.
  • Tenant details such as name, mobile number, emergency contact, Aadhaar number, company or college, joining date, rent, deposit, food preference, room, and bed assignment.
  • Tenant documents uploaded by the hostel owner or staff, such as tenant photo, Aadhaar front/back, employee ID, student ID, or agreement document.
  • Rent bills, payment records, receipt numbers, payment mode, and notes.
  • Expense and report information entered by users.

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide hostel and PG management features, including:

  • Authenticating owners and staff using mobile OTP.
  • Managing hostel records, rooms, beds, tenants, and tenant documents.
  • Generating rent bills, tracking paid/partial/pending rent, and creating receipts.
  • Showing dashboard summaries, occupancy, rent, expense, and profit reports.
  • Supporting staff access controlled by the hostel owner.

Data Storage and Service Providers

PGCopilot uses Supabase to provide authentication, database, row-level security, and document storage. Phone OTP delivery may use an SMS provider such as Twilio through Supabase authentication.

Tenant documents are stored in protected Supabase storage buckets. Access is restricted using application permissions and Supabase security policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share hostel or tenant data with third parties for advertising. Data may be processed by service providers required to operate the app, such as Supabase and SMS/OTP providers.

User Responsibilities

Hostel owners and staff are responsible for having permission to collect, upload, and manage tenant information and documents in PGCopilot. Users should enter only information they are authorised to manage.

Security

We use authentication, role-based access, row-level security, and protected storage access to help keep hostel data separated by owner, staff, and hostel. No system can guarantee complete security, so users should protect their login access and avoid sharing OTPs.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained while the hostel owner or authorised staff uses the app. To request deletion of account, hostel, tenant, or document data, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

PGCopilot is intended for hostel and PG owners, managers, and staff. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
