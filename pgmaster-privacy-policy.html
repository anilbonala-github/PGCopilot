Privacy Policy for PGMaster

Last updated: 15 June 2026

PGMaster is a Hostel/PG management application designed to help hostel and PG owners manage rooms, beds, tenants, rent, food expenses, expenses, and reports.

Information We Collect

PGMaster may allow hostel or PG owners to enter management information such as tenant names, mobile numbers, emergency contacts, Aadhaar numbers, company or college names, rent details, deposit details, room and bed assignments, food expenses, and property expense records.

The current version of the app does not send this information to our servers. Information entered in the app is used only to display and manage PG/hostel records inside the app.

How We Use Information

Information entered in PGMaster is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing hostel or PG rooms, beds, tenants, admissions, and vacates.
  • Tracking rent, pending payments, food expenses, and other property expenses.
  • Showing dashboard summaries and reports.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties in the current version of the app.

Data Storage and Security

The current version of PGMaster is intended for app-based management and demo use. Users should avoid entering sensitive personal information unless they have the right to collect and manage that information. If cloud sync, backups, payments, SMS, WhatsApp, or other integrations are added in future versions, this privacy policy will be updated before those features are released.

Children's Privacy

PGMaster is intended for hostel and PG owners or administrators. It is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

User Choices

Users can stop using the app at any time. If the app is uninstalled, app data stored locally on the device may be removed by the operating system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
